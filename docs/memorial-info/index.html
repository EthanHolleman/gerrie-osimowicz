Words of remembrance

Words of Remembrance for Geraldine Harriet Osimowicz By Michelle DeMars Holleman Thank you everyone who has joined us today to honor Geraldine “Gerrie” Harriet Osimowicz a caring daughter, sister, aunt, and friend. She passed on May 22, 2021, at the age of 88. I want to take a few minutes to reflect on Gerrie’s remarkable life. October 26, 1929, Sigmund Osimowicz and Harriet Zielinski, married and started a life together.
